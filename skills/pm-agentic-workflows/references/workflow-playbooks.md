# Workflow Playbooks

## 1) Discovery to PRD (2-5 days)

### Goal
Turn messy inputs into a decision-grade PRD draft.

### Steps
1. Synthesize user/business problem.
2. Define non-goals and constraints.
3. Propose solution options (at least 2).
4. Recommend one option with tradeoffs.
5. Define success metrics and instrumentation.
6. Produce PRD draft and open questions list.

### Exit criteria
- One recommended direction
- Measurable success criteria
- Named dependencies and risks

---

## 2) Weekly Control Tower (30-45 min cadence)

### Goal
Keep execution healthy and surface risks before deadlines slip.

### Steps
1. Summarize current sprint/phase status.
2. Compare planned vs actual progress.
3. Re-score top 5 risks (probability × impact).
4. Escalate blockers older than 3 business days.
5. Refresh next 2-week priorities.

### Exit criteria
- Updated RAG status
- Clear escalations and owners
- Updated 2-week action plan

---

## 3) Stakeholder Narrative Builder (20-30 min)

### Goal
Create role-specific updates from same source facts.

### Steps
1. Build single source-of-truth snapshot.
2. Generate three variants:
   - Executive summary
   - Engineering team update
   - Cross-functional partner update
3. Keep asks explicit and decision-ready.

### Exit criteria
- Consistent message across audiences
- At least one explicit ask/decision needed

---

## 4) Incident-to-Roadmap Learning Loop (same day)

### Goal
Convert incidents into roadmap-level prevention actions.

### Steps
1. Capture incident summary and user impact.
2. Identify systemic causes (process, tooling, architecture).
3. Define preventive backlog items.
4. Prioritize by risk-reduction ROI.
5. Recommend roadmap adjustments.

### Exit criteria
- At least one preventive investment candidate
- Clear owner and target milestone

---

## 5) Launch Readiness Gate (T-14 to launch)

### Goal
Decide launch readiness with objective go/no-go criteria.

### Steps
1. Confirm scope freeze and exceptions.
2. Validate reliability/performance/security checks.
3. Confirm docs, support, and communication plans.
4. Enumerate rollback plan and command chain.
5. Issue go/no-go recommendation with conditions.

### Exit criteria
- Binary recommendation (Go, Go-with-conditions, No-go)
- Conditions and mitigations documented
